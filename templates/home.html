A NOTE FROM CEO JACK

A little about us… My company’s most recent champagne moment was the heartwarming celebration we had for our 10th anniversary in July 2018. It was a milestone I have dreamed of achieving, and now that we have surpassed that mark, the team and I are dreaming even bigger! I truly believe that the continued success of All About Events comes from a few essential components. Staying true to our customer service standards, having a passionate team of dedicated, hardworking individuals that put our customers’ needs first, and the amazing client reviews and referrals that we receive has kept us growing in this industry. The company’s culture revolves around the power of relationships, as it has proven to be the root of existence. Relationships are formed and strengthened with everyone from our couples and their families as well as each event professional we have the pleasure of working with. Additionally, being willing to take risks or doing the unthinkable when needed and doing whatever it takes to ensure the event is executed successfully is what makes us different. Whether you are in need of event planning services, or are considering one of the truly unique venues we represent; our promise to you is that your vision and desires will be heard, your planning process will be enjoyable, and your event will leave you and your guests wanting more and loving life. We are at your disposal every step of the way and will ensure we leave you with a lasting impression.
